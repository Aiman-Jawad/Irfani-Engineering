About Us

Irfani Engineering Works was established approximately thirty years ago as an individual enterprise and later transitioned into a Private Limited Company. Initially, our focus was on manufacturing metal cans for the paints industry. Over time, we expanded our operations to include the production of plastic cans, incorporating advanced technologies such as Heat Transfer Labeling (HTL) and In-Mould Labeling (IML). Today, we are recognized as one of the leading can manufacturers in Pakistan.

Our customer base is extensive and includes all major multinational and local paint manufacturers in Pakistan. We are proud suppliers to AkzoNobel, Berger, Nippon, Kansai, Jotun, along with prominent local brands such as Brighto Paints, Diamond Paints, Master Paints, Gobi’s Paints, and Happilac Paints.

We have a state-of-the-art production facility, fully equipped with advanced machinery, workshops, and laboratories for in-house development and maintenance. Our infrastructure ensures precision, efficiency, and quality control at every stage of production.

At Irfani Engineering Works, we have a dedicated team of highly qualified and experienced professionals who oversee daily operations, ensuring seamless production and timely deliveries.

Our commitment is to provide high-quality cans at competitive prices while maintaining strict quality standards and timely deliveries. Through continuous improvement in systems, technology, and customer partnerships, we strive for excellence and long-term growth.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's largest plastic molding company and a globally recognized exporter within the next decade by delivering superior quality, innovative solutions, and cost-effective products. Guided by our commitment to research and development, we aim to serve our customers’ customers, adding value to their products and driving mutual success while contributing to sustainable industrial growth and fostering long-term partnerships worldwide.

Our Exports

Over the years, we have expanded our reach beyond borders, exporting high-quality products to neighboring countries. These international partnerships have allowed us to establish strong ties with global clients, enhancing our reputation as a reliable and innovative plastic manufacturing partner.

Customer Satisfaction at the Core

We understand that our customers rely on our products to meet their specific needs, and we take this responsibility seriously. Our quality assurance process involves close collaboration with our customers to understand their requirements and ensure that our products meet their expectations.
